This list identifies the sub-processors authorized to access customer data on behalf of Kat Digital.

This list identifies the sub-processors authorized to access customer data (including personal data contained therein). Any such sub-processors will be permitted to process customer data only to deliver the services Kat Digital has retained them to provide, and they are prohibited from using customer data for any other purpose. This list does not apply to services not yet in release. ## Digital Ocean, Inc - United States We use DigitalOcean to host our websites and other core internal infrastructure such as dev.kat.gg Personal Data may be transmitted to DigitalOcean via the use of our API end-points. You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy/ ## Cloudflare, Inc - United States We use Cloudflare for our website DNS management and content delivery network to cache website assets such as images, etc. Your Personal Data may be submitted through Cloudflare when you upload information to our websites. All information transmitted to our websites is secured by HTTPS. You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/ ## Proton Technologies AG - Switzerland We use ProtonMail for internal and external communications via email. All internal emails are end-to-end encrypted so only our employees have access to the information stored within emails. Emails sent to external recipients such as Gmail or Outlook may not store the information in an encrypted format. To ensure full security, we recommend you always contact us from another ProtonMail email address. We cannot confirm the security of your personal information if you are using services such as Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, etc. You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://protonmail.com/privacy-policy ## Stripe, Inc - United States We use Stripe as our payment processor for product billing and other invoicing activities. We only have access to specific information about your payment method such as the last 4 digits of your card number, the country your card was issued in, the billing address (if applicable) and card type e.g. pre-paid, etc. Stripe also stores a profile for each customer we have. We store information such as your real name, email address, invoices, billing events (timestamp of payments, etc). You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://stripe.com/us/privacy ## Slack Technologies, Inc. – United States We use Sleck for internal communications. Such internal communication may contain information about customers and partners. You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://slack.com/privacy-policy ## Mailgun Technologies, Inc - United States We use Mailgun to send automated emails from our websites such as password reset emails, notification emails, etc. These emails are stored in plain text format by Mailgun. We do not use Mailgun for any confidential correspondence. You can review their Privacy Policy here: https://www.mailgun.com/privacy-policy/
