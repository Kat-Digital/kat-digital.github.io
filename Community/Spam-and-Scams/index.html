# Spam and Scams Policy Do not post content if it fits any of the descriptions below. This applies as much to posts in our communities as it does to links to offsite content: - Posting large amounts of similar, repetitive and untargeted content - Content such as chain letters, or content that has no clear purpose - Giving topics on our forums a title that misleads users into viewing your post - this includes any "clickbait" titles - Posting links to Roblox assets that are verifiably malicious (including assets that contain obfuscated code or `require()` external modules with no clear purpose) - Content including, but not limited to, offering cash gifts, free items, or content asking users to run javascript or other software locally - Content that has been technically manipulated or doctored in a way that misleads users (beyond content taken out of context) and may pose a serious risk or harm to someone. ## Violations of this Policy If your content violates this policy, we’ll remove the content and send you a message to let you know. If this is your first time violating our Community Guidelines, you’ll get a warning with no penalty to your account. If it’s not, we’ll issue a strike against your account. If you get 3 strikes, your account will be terminated.
