# Impersonation Policy We don't allow users to impersonate others, whether that be other members of our communities or third parties. This is the case with users attempting to impersonate our staff, but is taken just as serious with users impersonating other members of our communities. **This includes, but is not limited to the following:** - Accounts with the same name and avatar as another user, with the only difference being a few characters (for example, an extra space or an 'O' being replaced with a 0, an i or L with a 1, etc.) - Creating an account using someone else's name (real name or username) and image, photo or other personal information. If you feel that you or another person is being impersonated, you can report the the account by contacting your Support group on the Developer Forum: https://dev.kat.gg/g/Support. ## Violations of this Policy If your content violates this policy, we’ll remove the content and send you a message to let you know. By violating this policy, you risk your account being terminated without any chance of appeal.
