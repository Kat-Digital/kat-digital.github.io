Kat Digital • Suicide and Self Injury Policy

We take the health and well-being of all our customers seriously. We don't support content that promotes self-harm.

# Suicide and Self Injury Policy We don't support content that promotes suicide or self harm. If you happen to find content that violates this policy, please report this to us immediately. It should be noted that we do allow our community to discuss their experiences with these topics, but we do not want content that may promote such behaviour or otherwise shock or disgust people. ## What to do if you need support If you're depressed or having thoughts of self harming or suicide, please know that there's always help our there and you're not alone with your feelings. It's completely understandable, many people turn to self harm or turn to thinking about suicide when suffering with painful emotions. We highly recommend talking to a specialist to help you find healthy, effective coping methods and help manage your difficult feelings. Please use these resources that are dedicated to helping those in need all around the world: - www.befrienders.org - suicide.org ## Guidelines for posting mental health topics We support people talking about their experiences however, we don't promote any content that promotes suicide or self harm. If your content fits any of the descriptions below, do not post it: - Providing instructions on self harm or suicide - Graphic content depicting self harm or suicide to shock or disgust people - Promoting or glorifying self harm or suicide This isn't by any means a complete list. Please make sure that your content does not in any way promote suicide or self harm. Mental health is a serious topic and we want to ensure our community is safe. ## Violations of this Policy If your content violates this policy, we’ll remove the content and send you a message to let you know. If this is your first time violating our Community Guidelines, you’ll get a warning with no penalty to your account. If it’s not, we’ll issue a strike against your account. If you get 3 strikes, your account will be terminated.
