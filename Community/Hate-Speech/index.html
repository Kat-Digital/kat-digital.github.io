Kat Digital • Hate Speech Policy

We don't allow any form of hate speech within our communities.

# Hate Speech Policy We do not support or otherwise allow content that is considered hate speech. We remove content that promotes violence or hatred against individuals or groups based on any of the following characteristics: - Age - Disability - Ethnicity - Gender Identity/Expression - Nationality - Race - Religion - Immigration Status - Sex/Gender - Sexual Orientation - Victims of any major violent event - Veteran Status **Do not post any content on our website(s) or social communities if the purpose of your content is to do one or more of the following things:** - Incite hatred against individuals or groups based on any of the characteristics stated above. - Encourage violence against individuals or groups based on any of the characteristics stated above. We do not allow threats and we treat implied calls for violence as real threats. - Content with the aim to shame, deceive or insult a minor. A minor is defined as a person under the legal age of majority. This usually means anyone under the age of 18. - Content that includes excessive name calling or malicious insults (such as insults about someone's race). ## Violations of this Policy If your content violates this policy, we may impose an immediate suspension of your account and any alternative accounts that may be connected to you.
